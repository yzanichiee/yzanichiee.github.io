Why Choose the STEM Strand?

Choosing the STEM strand offers numerous benefits, including:

  • Access to a growing job market
  • Diverse career opportunities
  • Development of critical analytical skills
  • Opportunities for innovation and creativity
  • Higher earning potential

By engaging in STEM, you'll be empowered to solve real-world problems and contribute positively to society.
